Meep · Last updated: April 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Meep ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Meep mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information. When you create an account, we collect information such as your name, email address, and any profile details you choose to provide.
  • Usage analytics. We collect information about how you interact with the App, including features used, session duration, and aggregated engagement metrics. This data helps us improve the App.
  • Device information and crash logs. We collect device identifiers, operating system version, app version, language preferences, and diagnostic data (including crash logs) to maintain reliability and security.
  • Content you create. Messages, posts, and other content you submit through social features of the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App and its social features.
  • Authenticate users and secure accounts.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand the App based on usage patterns.
  • Communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and support.
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and technical issues.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Other users. Content you post through social features may be visible to other users as designed by the App.
  • Service providers. With vendors who process data on our behalf (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics, crash reporting) under contractual confidentiality and security obligations.
  • Legal requirements. When required by law, subpoena, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Meep, our users, or others.
  • Business transfers. In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to this Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. You may request deletion of your account at any time (see Section 6).

5. Security

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below. You can also delete your account from within the App.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.
